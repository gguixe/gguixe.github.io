I've been messing around with Claude AI agents lately, and one experiment quickly turned into something actually useful: a tool to generate planet handouts for Mongoose Traveller 2e.

You can input a UWP code manually or generate one randomly, and the tool spits out a ready-to-use handout for your players. The results were surprisingly good, surprisingly fast — so instead of leaving it as a throwaway experiment, I've decided to keep going and manually tweak and polish it into something worth sharing.

It's still a work in progress, but feel free to try it out!

[Planet Handout Tool](https://gguixe.github.io/tools/planet-handout)
