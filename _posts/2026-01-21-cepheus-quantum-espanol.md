For those interested in science fiction and role-playing games, I just published a Spanish translation of Cepheus Quantum.
It's a very simple system based on Cepheus Engine (which is in turn based on Traveller), designed for one-shots, quick sessions, or as an introduction to this type of system.
There's very little material in Spanish for Cepheus — maybe someday I'll work up the motivation to translate the full SRD...
