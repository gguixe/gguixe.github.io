Background

Why Traveller?

Traveller is one of the greatest sci-fi TTRPGs ever made, built to capture the gritty, realistic feel of classic science fiction. You play scrappy merchants, mercenaries, and explorers just trying to survive in a vast, indifferent galaxy. Its legendary character creation, sandbox feel, huge range of starships and tech, and flexible skill system make it perfect for any sci-fi story you can imagine. If you love grounded, hard sci-fi, Traveller scratches that itch like nothing else.

These tools started as quick experiments for my own sessions and grew into something worth sharing. If they save you time at the table, consider buying me a coffee via the donations page.
